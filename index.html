Located near a major tech hub, the school offers hands-on courses in programming, electronics, and mechanical design, with students working on real-world projects like autonomous vehicles and robotic arms. Industry partnerships provide internships and mentorships, preparing students for careers in technology or advanced study in engineering fields.
